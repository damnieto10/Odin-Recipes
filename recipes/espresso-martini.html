Espresso Martini

Description

Enjoy this creamy espresso martini with a fantastic flavor that's not too sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 2 fluid ounces coffee-flavored liquer
  • 1 fluid ounce vanilla flavored vodka
  • 1 fluid ounce cream or milk

Steps

  1. Place ice into a cocktail shaker. Pour coffee liqueur, vodka, and cream over ice. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into 2 martini glasses.
